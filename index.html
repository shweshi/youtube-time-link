About

This tool generates URLs that start YouTube videos at specific times you specify.

When sharing a YouTube video and you want to start video at particular time, this tool lets you create links with start times.

Although the YouTube web version includes this feature, it's not available in the YouTube mobile app.

How to use?

  • Paste the YouTube Link.
  • Specify the start time in Hour, Minute or Seconds. You don't need to specify all of them. If you just want the video to start at 30th second you can just specify the seconds.
  • Click on "Generate" button.
  • Copy the generated URL and you can share it.
  • You can also play the video and verify before sharing the link.
